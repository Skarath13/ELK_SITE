Policies & Guidelines

Cancellation Policy

We require a minimum of 24 hours notice for all cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows will result in a charge of 50% of the service price.

Arrival Time

Please arrive 5-10 minutes before your appointment. Late arrivals may result in reduced service time while being charged for the full service price.

Health & Safety

Please reschedule if you have any eye infections, recent eye surgery, or active allergies. Your safety is our top priority.

Payment

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and digital payments. Full payment is required at the time of service.

Refund Policy

Due to the personalized nature of our services, we do not offer refunds. However, we're happy to address any concerns about your service.
